About Us

We are a family run business, operating since 2018. Located in the heart of the Irish Midlands, we have easy access to the entire country allowing us to offer our service throughtout Ireland.

We aim to offer you a luxury tailored experience, and want to make your experience with us the best it can possibly be. Our chauffeurs are professional, courteous and will assist in any way possible to make your day stress free and memorable.

We specialise in wedding car hire, but we are also available for any and all other special occasions. Just get in touch and we would be happy to help.

About The Car

The car is a 1987 Rolls-Royce Spirit Mark III. Just 211 were ever built, making this an exceptionally rare car. Masterfully crafted from front to back, it is the epitome of luxury motoring.

Leading with The Spirit of Ecstasy on the bonnet and powered along by a 6.75L V8, nothing comes close to the feeling of cruising in this car.

The most coveted seats in any Rolls-Royce are of course the back seats. With an abundance of leg room you can rest assured that there will be plenty of room for you, no matter how large the dress.

We spare absolutely no expense in maintaining the car to make sure the car is as reliable as it is comfortable. After all, we strive for perfection in everything you do.
