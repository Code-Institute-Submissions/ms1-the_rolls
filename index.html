What We Do

We are Ireland's premier chauffeur driven classic car hire company.

We offer our service throughout Ireland and specialise in offering our first class service at weddings. We also offer our service for all other special occasions.

We tailor our service around your day.

If you choose to book with us, we keep the rest of the day clear so that we can focus on your day, and your day alone. Our aim is to make your experience completely stress free and give you the Rolls Royce experience.
